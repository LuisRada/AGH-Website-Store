What do we offer?

Studies

The university’s offer includes the AGH University Doctoral School with 16 disciplines and more than 100 professional courses. We currently offer 23 degree programmes taught entirely in English (first-cycle = Bachelor’s, second-cycle = Master’s, and specialties) and more than 100 courses available each semester within the 'AGH University International Courses' base of electives.
